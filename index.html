Heritage & Artistry

Sculpted to Perfection

For generations, the name Sadiq has stood for uncompromising attention to luxury. Re-imagined as Maison Sadique, our boutique atelier hand-assembles watch complications utilizing 18k rose gold, surgical-grade stainless steel, and anti-reflective double-domed sapphire crystals.

We operate in highly limited production runs to guarantee absolute exclusivity and preserve the extreme quality expected of luxury collectors.
